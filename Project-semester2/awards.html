Awards

The Institute of Public Administration runs a series of awards in Tasmania to recognise and enhance public administration and management.


National Fellow Award

To be made a National Fellow is the highest accolade for IPAA members. This prestigious award recognises the recipient's outstanding contribution to the practice and study of public administration.

Program Workplace Project Awards

IPAA Tasmania presents a prize to the Public Sector Management Program graduate with the highest mark in the work based project at the annual Public Sector Management Program graduation held at Parliament House.

Public Sector Excellence Awards

The Public Sector Excellence Awards recognise outstanding individuals and teams who are leading the way in public administration and making a positive difference for the Tasmanian community.

Student Awards

IPAA Tasmania, in conjunction with UTAS School of Social Sciences, award three prizes each year to the highest achievers in policy units at the University of Tasmania.

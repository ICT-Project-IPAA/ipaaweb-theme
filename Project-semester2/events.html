Events

Our events provide a place for the public sector to come together to learn, debate, share experiences and explore ideas. IPAA hosts seminars, keynote addresses, networking functions and briefings. Click on the links below for more information about our upcoming events.

The public face of the public sector

Friday 29 July 2016

Join Asscociate Professor Dennis Grube, University of Tasmania; Jacqui Allen, Deputy Secretary, Department of State Growth; and the Human face of Centrelink, Hank Jongen PSM, as they discuss the emerging trend of public sector leaders taking a more active role in public debate.

A 'virtual' Address to the APS by Prime Minister Malcolm Turnbull

Wednesday, 20 April 2016

Prime Minister Turnbull will provide an Address to the APS at an IPAA ACT event in the Great Hall, Parliament House, Canberra. Sign up to live stream the event.

2016: an election year with Dr John Hewson

Monday 27 June 2016

Scheduled on the Monday before the federal election, this forum will be a timely analysis of the recent performance of Australian governments, and the challenges facing the public sector in supporting the incoming government, whatever persuasion it may be.

Public Sector Excellence Awards

The Public Sector Excellence Awards recognise outstanding individuals and teams who are leading the way in public administration and making a positive difference for the Tasmanian community. David Nicholson, President of IPAA Tasmania Division, announced the winners at the annual State of the Service presentation held at Commission for the Conservation of Antarctic Marine Living Resources on 16 December 2015. This year's winners are as follows.

2015

President's award for excellence - Phil Foulston, Department of Premier and Cabinet/Department of Justice.

Public sector professional of the year (joint winner) - Alison Lai, Department of Premier and Cabinet/Department of Health and Human Services.

Public sector professional of the year (joint winner) - Leon Smith, Tasmania Fire Service.

Public sector young professional of the year (winner) - Karla Williams, Department of Primary Industries, Parks, Water and Environment.

Public sector young professional of the year (runner up) - Sarah Jane Rigney, Australian Antarctic Division.

Best practice in the public sector (winner) - Tasmanian Arts Guide, Arts Tasmania, Department of State Growth.

Best practice in the public sector (highly commended) - edi, the Education Information Portal, Department of Education.

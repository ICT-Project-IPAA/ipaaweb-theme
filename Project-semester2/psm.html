Workplace projects-Public Sector Management Program

IPAA Tasmania presents a prize to the Public Sector Management Program graduate with the highest mark in the work based project at the annual Public Sector Management Program graduation held at Parliament House.

At the 2015 graduation, IPAA Tasmania President David Nicholson (right) presented the IPAA award to Angela Fooks (left) from the Australian Government Department of Health for her project - Change Management: Leading Organisation Learning.
